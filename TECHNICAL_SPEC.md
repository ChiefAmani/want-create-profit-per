PROJECT NAME: Profit Per Job Calculator

FILE TREE:
.
|-- Profit Per Job Calculator.xlsx
|-- TECHNICAL_SPEC.md

TECH STACK:
- Microsoft Excel (Version 2016 or newer recommended for full feature compatibility)

FEATURE REQUIREMENTS:
1. Price vs Labour Breakdown:
   - Input fields for total job price, hourly labor rate, and estimated labor hours.
   - Calculation of total labor cost and percentage of labor cost relative to job price.
2. Chemical Cost Tracking:
   - Input fields for chemicals used per job (e.g., name, unit cost, quantity used).
   - Calculation of total chemical cost per job.
3. Callback Cost Impact:
   - Input fields for estimated callback hours and associated labor/material costs.
   - Calculation of total callback cost and its impact on job profitability.
4. Break Even Calculator:
   - Input fields for fixed monthly costs (e.g., rent, insurance, vehicle payments) and variable costs per job (labor, chemicals, other direct costs).
   - Calculation of the number of jobs required to cover all costs.
5. "Are You Undercharging" Calculator:
   - Input fields for desired profit margin, total job costs (labor, chemicals, callbacks, overhead allocation).
   - Suggests a minimum profitable price based on desired margin and costs.

ADDITIONAL PROPOSED FEATURES:
1. Automated Pricing System:
   - Based on area size, cleaner count, and productivity rate.
   - Estimates supply costs using real product usage and markup percentage.
2. Revenue Per Cleaner Per Day Tracking:
   - Input for cleaner hours and total revenue to calculate efficiency.
3. Client Retention Rate Tracking:
   - Simple tracking of repeat clients to understand long-term value.
4. Average Job Value:
   - Calculates the average revenue generated per job over a period.
5. Billable Hours Utilization:
   - Tracks the percentage of paid hours versus total available working hours.
